R & D Overview

Focused on efficient, optimised, and durable methods.

We have our research and development lab located in Sydney. It is our ongoing effort to find efficient, optimised, and durable methods and materials in construction using 3D concrete printing.

Our work also includes researching how to improve 3D concrete printing methods, material behaviour, robotic accuracy, and construction workflows.

Research Pillars

Core areas of development.

01

Material Performance

Studying concrete mixes, material behaviour, strength, and print suitability for advanced construction applications.

02

Robotic Accuracy

04

Sustainable Methods

Development Process

From research to construction-ready methodology.

02

03
